### Introduction
Salary is a core component that most job applicants consider while seeking for new roles. However, 
it is not a cake-breaking moment to decide what salary to provide when asked for your desired salary for the new role.
And therefore, this project aims at providing you with an approximate figure to provide based on your 
basic variables such as age, years of experience, role applying for, and level of education. 
On the other hand, it is crucial to understand how salary associates with various factors. As such, 
this report provides you with basic insights obtained from my analysis.
